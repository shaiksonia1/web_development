10 MOVIES
TO WATCH WHEN BORED

Baby's Day Out

Plot Synopsis: Baby's Day Out is a family comedy film about a wealthy couple’s infant son, who three bumbling criminals kidnap. Despite their best efforts to keep the baby under control, the kidnappers quickly lose him. They spend the rest of the film frantically searching for him in various locations around the city.

Dumb and Dumber

Plot Synopsis: Lloyd Christmas (Jim Carrey) and Harry Dunne (Jeff Daniels) are two dim-witted best friends. They set out on a road trip from Rhode Island to Aspen, Colorado, to return a briefcase full of money to its owner. Along the way, they encounter a series of comedic misadventures and obstacles, including a woman named Mary Swanson (Lauren Holly). Who they mistakenly believe is in trouble and set out to rescue.

Hangover

Plot Synopsis: The Hangover is a comedy film that follows the story of three friends, Phil (Bradley Cooper), Stu (Ed Helms), and Alan (Zach Galifianakis). They travel to Las Vegas for a bachelor party to celebrate their friend Doug’s (Justin Bartha) upcoming wedding. However, after a wild night of drinking and partying, the three friends wake up the next morning with no memory of the previous night and find Doug missing.

Snatch

Plot Synopsis: Snatch is a British crime comedy film that follows a series of interlocking stories involving a diverse cast of characters in the London criminal underworld. The film centers around a stolen diamond and the various groups of people trying to get their hands on it.

The Dictator

Plot Synopsis: The Dictator is a political satire comedy film that follows the story of Admiral General Aladeen (Sacha Baron Cohen). The ruthless dictator of the fictional North African country of Wadiya. When Aladeen’s plans to develop nuclear weapons attract the United Nations’ attention, he travels to New York to address the General Assembly. However, his trip takes a surprising turn when he is kidnapped and replaced with a lookalike, leaving him stranded in New York City without any resources or help.
